Boardgame Dice

This is a simple website that refers to the free iOS app "boardgame dice" it is designed to make playing board games that use two regular size sided dice a little easier.

During many boardgames, such as the Settlers of Catan, it can be annoying when an unlikely number such as 2, or 12 comes up a number of times. Thus the full gammut of 36 possible options are rolled in advance and then shown one at a time until 31 have been shewn. At which point the process will repeat with a new randomised set of rolls.

The game will additionally keep track of who's turn it is to play.

If you enable the "Voice Control" (Settings > Accessibility > Voice Control > Voice Control) then you will able to issue the voice command "Press Roll". To cause the next roll to occur.

Be aware that the the Voice Control involves your phone listening to everything so you might want to switch this off when not using the boardgame dice.
